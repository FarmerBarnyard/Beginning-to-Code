Beef Nachos

Beef nachos are a popular and delicious dish made with tortilla chips, seasoned ground beef, melted cheese, and various toppings. The dish starts with a base of crispy tortilla chips, providing a satisfying crunch. The chips are then topped with seasoned ground beef, cooked with spices like taco seasoning, garlic, and onions, adding a savory and meaty flavor. A generous amount of melted cheese, such as cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend of cheeses, is added on top of the beef. Additional toppings can be customized based on personal preference, including diced tomatoes, sliced jalapenos, black olives, sour cream, guacamole, and salsa. The nachos are often baked or broiled in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the toppings are heated through. Once cooked, beef nachos are served hot and can be enjoyed as an appetizer, snack, or even a main course. Beef nachos are a crowd-pleasing treat that combines the flavors of savory beef, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings for a delicious and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

Nachos

  • 2 tbs vegetable oil
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • Salt, to taste
  • Tortilla chips
  • Shredded cheese
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 1/3 cup pickled jalapenos
  • sliced black olives
  • 3 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 3 baby capsicums

To Garnish

  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • Hot sauce
  • Lime, cut wedges

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 240 C
  2. In a large saucepan, add vegetable oil, beef mince and cook until beef is browned.
  3. Once browned, add salsa, taco seasoning and cook for 1-2 minutes, remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Add tortilla chips onto a baking tray, spread a thin layer of shredded cheese, then layer seasoned beef.
    5. \
  5. Then add refried beans and baby peppers before baking for 3-5 minutes or until the cheese is completely melted.
  6. Top with black olives, jalapenos, capsicum and scallions.
  7. In a small bowl, mix sour cream, salsa, hot sauce and lime juice to serve alongside nachos.
